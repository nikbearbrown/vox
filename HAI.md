# HAI — Humanitarians AI

**Audience.** Busy, mid-career practitioners applying AI in social-impact / humanitarian
work who need to get productive with AI *fast*.

**Narration voice.** `ELEVENLABS_VOICE_HUMANITARIANS`.

**Register — Pragmatist.** Here's the method, here's when to use it, here's when it
fails, here's how to apply it — efficient, no personality tax, built for someone who
needs to solve a problem now. See `voices/pragmatist/VOICE.md`.

**Why this register.** "Busy" + "get good quickly" is Pragmatist's mandate: method
first, no story to chase and no first-principles detour when you're slammed. It's the
**express cut** of the same material the default (Teardown) treats in full — the same
professional, a smaller time budget. **The critical move for AI:** the *when **not** to
use it / where it fails* part is the main event, not a footnote — with AI, competence
lives in knowing when the output is wrong, and in high-stakes humanitarian work a
confident misuse is worse than being slow.

**Palette — muted editorial** (Economist / FT-adjacent) on the cream base. CVD-safe by
pairing the semantic accents across warm/cool: **petrol teal `#1F4E5F` = good/kept**,
**burnt orange `#E4572E` = bad/lost** (blue-vs-orange survives red-green blindness),
**navy `#29335C` = structure**, **amber `#F3A712` = highlighter** (fill only), **sage
`#A8C686` = a human/growth tertiary**. Two blues are in play — keep navy (structure) and
petrol (good) in clearly different roles so they don't read as one. **Color law**
applies; on cream, amber + sage are low-contrast (fills + labels only).
Tokens: `aspects/remotion-pass/remotion/src/tokens/humanitarians.ts`.

**Signature tangent — the Irreducibly-Human tangent** (0–1 per video, *only* on a clear
opportunity). HAI is fundamentally about *when to use AI, when not to, and what is
irreducibly human*. When a clean line appears, it may take one aside: *"this the AI does
well; this is the human's, and can't be handed off."* A decision boundary, not a sermon.
Line isn't clear → silence.

**Pedagogy.** Decision-oriented and evidence-forward; the AI / human boundary is the
through-line; restraint over spectacle; get the practitioner productive — and safe —
fast. The whole audience is organized around *use / don't-use / irreducibly human*.
